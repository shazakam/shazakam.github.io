I consider Oslo International School to be a second home. Not only because I have been attending at various stages in my life but mostly due to the people I encountered and consequently befriended. My fondest memories were created here, from the playground to teenage parties to relationships old and new being formed to exam stressed periods induced with dangerous amounts of caffeine. The people I met and memories they have given me have helped define who I am and also who I wish to be. My teachers spurred my academic interests which consequently led me to taking four higher level subjects in IB when the regular is three. I excelled in all my classes and in particular Physics which has always had a place in my heart since I was a child.
