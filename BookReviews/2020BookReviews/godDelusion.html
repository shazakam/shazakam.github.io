The God Delusion By Richard Dawkins

The purpose of this book is to present the arguments against religion and why superstition and beliefs not based in reason and logic hurt society. Dawkings touches ideas and social norms as the (and he shows questionable) respect for religion when it comes to laws and governments (for example tax exemption). Dawkins writes succinclty with logic, brutal honesty and with making this a truly wonderful book to read but also to learn from.

Rating: 9/10 Illogical proofs of God
