Dante's Divine Comedy:Inferno translated by Mark Musa

Inferno is the first installment to the Divine Comedy descrbing the Piligrims journey through but also his own self discovery, as well as the various sins found within humanity according to Catholicism. The language is rich with symbollism, metaphors, imagery and references to people in Dante's life/history but also to other great poets and thinkers of the Antiquity. Musa's annotations and explanations are presented wonderfully and truly let you jump straight into the material without independent research.

Rating: 6/7 Deadly Sins
