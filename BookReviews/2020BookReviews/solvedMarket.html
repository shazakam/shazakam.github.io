The Man Who Solved The Market by Gregory Zuckerman

This biography explores the life of Jim Simons, a mathematician who started one of the worlds most succesful hedgefunds pioneering algorithmic investing and quantitive finance. Zuckerman does a great job at displaying the fortitude of character that Simons displays, the relationships involved and the methods used in the fund. The writing is captivating and will be of interest to people involved in finance.

Rating: 9/10 Quants
