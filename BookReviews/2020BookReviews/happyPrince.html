The Happy Prince and Other Short Stories by Oscar Wilde

A beautiful set of short stories written by yours truly, Mr Wilde. Wilde has such a unqiue manner to describe everyday objects and most importantly luxuries and beauties. The theme of eauty being compared to moral/inner beauty (and its falsehood) is resounding and ever present as it is one Wilde's most toyed with ideas (not only in the short stories but also his novel). This book is written for children, however, that does not diminish its importance, as moral stories do not only hold true for children but also for adults. Wilde is able to weave melancholic yet sweet tales with mesmerising imagery while also sending a moral message. This book is perfect for anyone who wishes for a story to throw you into a world of odd fantasies and tales, if only for a moment.

Rating: 10/10 Happy Princes
