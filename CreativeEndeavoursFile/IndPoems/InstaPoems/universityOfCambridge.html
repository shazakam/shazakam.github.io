Commentary

In 2018 as I spent two weeks in Cambridge for an engineering summer camp to figure out what I wanted to study. I met a whole spectrum of new and had a great time. I hope the poem shows the joy which I gained from my experience there.

Univeristy of Cambridge Summer camp

How two weeks flew,
And embedded a drawing of all of you,
on my little brain,
Which was so thankful for such a gain,
Of mkaing new friends so great,
All joined together by fate,
Who knows what the future holds,
Please remeber to stay bold,
Venture forth, into a world unknown,
One which you have shown,
To be wonderous and thunderous,
Full of laughter and joviality,
Something that helps with the clarity,
Of happiness.

With friends so many,
And enemies so few,
How could I ever forget about,
All of you.
