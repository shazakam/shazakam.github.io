Commentary

My three good friends, Justin, Rafa and Breno invited me to join along on their family trip to Israel and Jordan. We had a smashing time, Exploring the countries vast and ancient history, eating amazing food and laughing in good company.

Israel Trip

Is it not in human nature to quench the thirst for the thrills of life?
One filled with strife,
Cut deeply by a knife,
Can be soothed and healed by friends,
Without ends.

To a holy land,
Filled with sand,
Did I travel with this merry band.

Three wise, all greater than the sum of their parts,
Reveled in showing me their farts.

One with the great power of comedy and procrastination,
And has somehow not ended in damnation,
Another, an occasional gambler with a sharp tongue,
But a heart warmer than the fiery heat of the sun,
The last timid and occasionally oblivious to the surrounding nature,
But a loving kind soul is found within this creature.

When the three,
bathed in the Dead Sea,
Their nipples burnt as if stung by a thousand bees.

The miseries of life do cut deep,
And blood does seep,
But there is no need to weep,
When you have friends without ends,
An unexpected journey calls,
Justin put mud on his balls.
