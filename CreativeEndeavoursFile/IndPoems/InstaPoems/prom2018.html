Commentary

Well this was quite a night. We started it at my friends house drinking copious amounts of alcohol as well as getting dressed in our evening attire. We would then, in a group, stumble to the venue to meet our dates (surprisingly mine would, later that year, become my first girlfriend), We then danced all night long. Overall, quite a smashing evening.

Prom 2018

I went to school,
I played the fool,
I ended up here,
I know not where.

But does it matter?
When there is so much chatter,
To fill the night with song,
For an evening so long.

With such phenomenal people,
Who are never evil,
'Twas a night to remember,
And a night to forget,
Because I ended up collapsing,
In someone else's bed.
