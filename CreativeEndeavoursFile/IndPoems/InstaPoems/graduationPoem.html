Commentary

Captured in this post was potentially one of my happiest moments in my life so far. I was proud of what I had achieved, I had a wonderful sphere of friends all of whom I shared special moments throughout highschool, I was in love for the first time, I was excited for the future that I was aspiring to reach and I had a loving family to support me through it all.

IB Graduation Poem

As I sit tinkering,
Listening to all the bickering the world has to offer,
I now know that I have a coffer,
Full of love and joviality.

In this coffer I take the fun I require,
To avoid all daily banality,
Some may find these words to be insanity,
A most horrendous calamity,
But to surround oneself with these folk so kind,
it's difficult not to be in such a frame of mind.

I know not where to go,
who to be,
What to do,
A never ending mold of messiness,
I confess.

Atleast I know where I have been,
Who I have been,
And What I've done.

But most importantly, I know the people I did all this with...
