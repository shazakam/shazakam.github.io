Commentary

Sometimes everything can seem so hopeless, futile and energy consuming, that you reach a point where all you can do is simply accept that what is happening is happening or as so succinclty put by the internet, it iiizzzzz what it izzzzz. There is no shame in giving up if you have tried with all your effort, but to be able to smile at the end of a loss is an abilitiy few possess.

So Be It

I stand sole at the gate,
My brothers gone to deaths embrace,
I see the fires of hell declaring my fate,
So Be It.

I feel the sky fall,
Held on my shoulders as Atlas once did,
I strain under its weight,
So Be It.

I am pulled to the depths,
The abyss welcomes me while I fight,
I succumb,
So Be It.

I am no match for them,
Bloodied, bruised and blind,
And I retaliate,
So Be It.

I gave all my heart,
Soaked in liquids of red,
Bursting, shrapnel pieces left behind,
So Be It.

Look at me with pity or disgust,
Gaze upon my corpse, my shallow husk,
Shed no tears, no love for me,
So Be It.

What else can a man do?
To stand upright in the face of doom,
To hold his head over his shoulders past celestial gloom,
To reach out of the water in hope for a friend,
To be beaten and laugh till the end,
To hold out the pieces of your shattered heart.

He shouts, “I am gone”,
He whispers, “I can’t look up”,
He mumbles, “I need you”,
He sighs, “I have lost”,
He cries, “I Love you”.

What more can a man do?
Than to look despair into its eyes,
With a smile and a wink,
He declares “So Be It”.
