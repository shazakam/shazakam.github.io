Commentary

There can be a certain comfort in melancholy. Although it is usually connotated in quite a negative light, it can provide room for deeper self reflection.

Oh, How I missed the darkness…

Oh, How I missed the darkness,
It’s comforting sound of inexistence,
An eloquent negotiation with tragedy,
The darkness is escorted by my melancholy.

Oh, How I missed the darkness.

I feel her in my essence,
Encroaching and embracing me tightly,
Sweet cold kiss of her lips yet to appear,
Not here, not there, but simply everywhere.

Oh, How I missed the darkness.
