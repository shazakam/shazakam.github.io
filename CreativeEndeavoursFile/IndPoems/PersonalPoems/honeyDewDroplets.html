Commentary

Everybody has their own secret pleasure which may lie unknown to the world and even those cloest to you. Due to their hidden nature they come only in little sweet droplets, away from the prying eyes of others.

Honeydew Droplets

I want to wake up to Honeydew droplets,
Some of which, no one ever forgets,
About how tiresome life gets,
When you long for Honeydew droplets.

They dance on the leaves of trees,
Ones which no one but me can see,
How the bees never seize,
In the endless breeze of time,
To produce my Honeydew droplets.
